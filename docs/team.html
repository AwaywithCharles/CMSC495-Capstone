Meet the Team

Charles Bostwick | Team Lead

As the team leader, Charles was instrumental in guiding the team throughout the project. He drafting all documentation, selected the tech stack, provided troubleshooting for tech issues, and steered the project's direction. His significant technical contribution was transitioning the entire game codebase from Godot to JavaScript.

Jade Pearl | Game Designer/Developer, Communication Specialist

Jade spearheaded the initial development of Godot, crafting foundational game mechanics and ensuring they aligned with our design ethos. Her organizational skills were pivotal in setting up our communication channels and aligning the team.

Ada Truong | Frontend Developer, Game Developer, UI/UX Developer

Ada added a Minesweeper game, expanding our project's scope. Her role was also critical in defining the website's visual and functional style. She designed the initial themes and was responsible for the website's responsive and accessible design.

Holland Brawner | User Experience & User Interface Developer, Game Developer

Holland's focus on constructing a semantic and accessible HTML framework was a testament to our collaborative spirit. His partnership with Ada and Robyn was vital in implementing responsive and intuitive web designs that catered to diverse devices and user needs, a crucial aspect of our project's success.

Robyn Cohen | User Interface & User Experience Developer

Robyn significantly contributed to the Figma designs, creating reusable components and defining the project's visual identity. Her expertise ensured the project's designs were both functional and aesthetically pleasing across various devices.
